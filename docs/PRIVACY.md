# StudyArena — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-10_

StudyArena ("we", "us") operates the StudyArena mobile and web application (the "App"). This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. We built StudyArena to be privacy-respecting by default.

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** a username, email address, country, and (optionally) a profile picture you upload. If you sign in with **Apple** or **Google**, we receive a basic identifier and, where you allow it, your email.
- **Activity data:** study sessions (subject, duration, timestamps), XP, coins, rating, rank, streaks, achievements, friends, and battle results — the data needed to run the competitive features.
- **Device data:** for native notifications we store a local notification preference on your device. We do not collect advertising identifiers.

In offline/demo mode, your data is stored **only on your device** (local storage) and is not transmitted to any server.

## How we use your information

- To provide core features: profiles, leaderboards, ranked play, streaks, achievements, and social activity.
- To send **local streak reminders** if you enable them (scheduled on-device; no third-party push service is required).
- To maintain fair competition and operate seasonal rankings.

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do not use it for third-party advertising.

## Data sharing

Certain information is shown to other users by design — your username, country, avatar, rank, and public activity (e.g. "completed a 2h Mathematics session"). You control your username and avatar in **Settings → Edit Profile**.

When connected to a backend (Supabase), data is processed by that provider on our behalf under their security and data-processing terms.

## Notifications

Streak reminders are **opt-in** and scheduled locally on your device. You can turn them off any time in **Settings → Streak reminders**, or in your device's system notification settings.

## Data retention & deletion

You can **permanently delete your account and all associated data** at any time from **Profile → Settings → Delete account**. Deletion removes your profile, stats, streak, battles, and achievements. In demo mode this clears all locally stored data immediately.

## Children's privacy

StudyArena is intended for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. the EU/EEA under GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
